# Welcome to Computer Programming the HARD Way
The title is a bit of a misnomer, and if you really interested in becoming a computer programmer, no matter what discipline, this

I decided to build this website and resource out of frustration from the negative experience I had with the instructor of two of my courses in my first semester of 2-year computer programming diploma.
This is meant to supplement the current educational requirements at Seneca Polytechnic while also noting what things are not important or flat out wrong, and what skills we can build on. There will also be other helpeful recommendations, tips, and even games to help make your experience at Seneca Polytechnic a more encouraging place for a budding programmer. More on that later.
